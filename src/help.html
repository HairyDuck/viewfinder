General Usage

Viewfinder allows you to open and view images directly, with options for zoom, rotation, and navigation.

  • Zoom: Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out, or the toolbar buttons.
  • Rotate: Click the rotate button to rotate the image by 90Â° each time.
  • Navigation: Navigate through images using the left and right arrow keys or the toolbar buttons.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Arrow Left: Go to the previous image.
  • Arrow Right: Go to the next image.
  • Cmd/Ctrl + O: Open an image file.
  • Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + I: Toggle Developer Tools.

FAQ

  • How do I zoom? Use the mouse wheel, or click the zoom buttons in the toolbar.
  • Can I open folders? No, you can only open individual images at the moment.
